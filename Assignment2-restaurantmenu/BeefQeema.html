Beef Qeema

Beef qeema, also known as minced beef, is a popular dish in South Asian and Middle Eastern cuisines. It is made by finely chopping or grinding beef into small pieces, creating a versatile base for a variety of dishes. Beef qeema is typically cooked with a blend of aromatic spices, such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala, along with ingredients like onions, garlic, and ginger. This combination of flavors infuses the beef with a rich and savory taste. Beef qeema can be prepared in several ways, including as a curry, kebabs, or as a filling for samosas, wraps, or pies. It is often enjoyed with rice, roti, or naan. Beef qeema is a hearty and flavorful dish that showcases the versatility of beef in traditional cuisines.

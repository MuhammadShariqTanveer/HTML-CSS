Chicken Qofta

Chicken qofta is a savory and aromatic dish that features flavorful minced chicken formed into meatballs and cooked in a rich and spiced gravy. It is a popular delicacy in Middle Eastern, Mediterranean, and South Asian cuisines. To prepare chicken qofta, ground chicken is combined with a variety of ingredients such as finely chopped onions, garlic, ginger, fresh herbs like cilantro or parsley, and a blend of spices including cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder. The mixture is then shaped into small meatballs, also known as qoftas.

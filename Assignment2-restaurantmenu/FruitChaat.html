Fruit Chaat

Fruit chaat is a refreshing and colorful dish that showcases a delightful combination of fresh fruits tossed together with tangy and sweet flavors. It is a popular snack and street food in South Asian cuisine, particularly in India and Pakistan. To prepare fruit chaat, a variety of seasonal fruits are selected and diced into bite-sized pieces. Common fruits used in fruit chaat include apples, bananas, oranges, grapes, pomegranate seeds, pineapple, and melons. The choice of fruits may vary based on availability and personal preference.

Chana Chaat

Channa chaat is a popular street food and snack in South Asian cuisine, particularly in India and Pakistan. It is a tangy and flavorful dish made with chickpeas (channa) combined with a variety of spices, chutneys, and other ingredients. To prepare channa chaat, boiled chickpeas are mixed with finely chopped onions, tomatoes, and green chilies. It is then seasoned with a blend of spices such as chaat masala, roasted cumin powder, red chili powder, and black salt for a savory and tangy flavor. Freshly squeezed lemon juice adds a refreshing citrusy kick.

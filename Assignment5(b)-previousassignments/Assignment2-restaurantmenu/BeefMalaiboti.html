Beef Malaiboti

Beef Malaiboti is a delectable dish that blends the flavors of tender beef and a creamy, flavorful sauce. Originating from the Indian subcontinent, it is a dish known for its richness and aromatic profile. To prepare Beef Malaiboti, boneless beef pieces are marinated in a mixture of yogurt, spices, and herbs. The marinated beef is then cooked in a creamy sauce made with ingredients such as cashew paste, cream, onions, garlic, ginger, and a blend of spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala. The sauce is simmered until it thickens and coats the chicken, creating a luscious and velvety texture.

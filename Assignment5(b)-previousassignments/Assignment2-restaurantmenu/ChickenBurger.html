Chicken Burger

A chicken burger is a mouthwatering and popular sandwich that features a juicy chicken patty nestled between two burger buns. It is a delicious alternative to traditional beef burgers and offers a lighter and leaner option. To make a chicken burger, a chicken patty is typically made from ground or minced chicken, combined with various seasonings and ingredients. These may include breadcrumbs, eggs, onions, garlic, herbs, and spices like paprika, cumin, or chili powder. The mixture is shaped into patties and then cooked by grilling, pan-frying, or baking until they are cooked through and golden brown.

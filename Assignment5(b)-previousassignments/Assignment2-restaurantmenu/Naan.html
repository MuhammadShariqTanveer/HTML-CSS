Naan

Naan is a traditional flatbread that is popular in South Asian and Middle Eastern cuisines. It is a versatile bread that is commonly enjoyed alongside a variety of dishes such as curries, kebabs, and tandoori preparations. To make naan, a dough is prepared using flour, yeast, salt, sugar, yogurt, and sometimes milk or water. The dough is kneaded until it becomes soft and elastic. It is then left to rest and rise, allowing the yeast to activate and create air pockets in the dough.

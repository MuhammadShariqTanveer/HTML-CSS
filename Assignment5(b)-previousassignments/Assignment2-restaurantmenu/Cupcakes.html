Cupcakes

Cupcakes are delightful individual-sized cakes that are enjoyed by people of all ages. They are a popular treat for birthdays, parties, or simply as a sweet indulgence. Cupcakes are made from a simple batter that is baked in small, paper-lined cups, giving them their distinctive shape. To make cupcakes, a basic cake batter is prepared by combining ingredients such as flour, sugar, butter, eggs, milk, and flavorings like vanilla extract. The ingredients are mixed together until a smooth and creamy batter is formed. Variations of cupcakes can include additional ingredients such as cocoa powder for chocolate flavor, fruit purees for fruity cupcakes, or spices for a touch of warmth and complexity.

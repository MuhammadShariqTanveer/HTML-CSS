Chest Tikka

Chicken chest tikka refers to a variation of the popular chicken tikka dish where specifically the chicken breast is used. It follows a similar process of marinating chicken breast pieces in a spiced yogurt mixture and then grilling or baking them to perfection. The marinade typically consists of a blend of spices, such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, paprika, chili powder, garlic, ginger, and lemon juice. The chicken breasts are then cooked until they are tender and have a slightly charred exterior, resulting in a juicy and flavorful dish. Chicken chest tikka can be enjoyed as an appetizer, a protein-rich addition to salads, or as a filling in wraps and sandwiches. It offers a leaner option while retaining the aromatic flavors and succulent texture of traditional chicken tikka.

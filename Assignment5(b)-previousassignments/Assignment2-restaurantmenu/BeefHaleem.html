Beef Haleem

Beef haleem is a rich and flavorful dish that is popular in South Asian and Middle Eastern cuisines. It is a thick and hearty stew made with a combination of beef, lentils, wheat, and spices. Haleem is a labor-intensive dish that requires slow cooking over several hours to achieve its unique texture and taste. To prepare beef haleem, beef pieces are cooked with lentils, wheat grains, and a blend of spices such as ginger, garlic, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder. The mixture is simmered and stirred continuously until the ingredients break down and blend together, resulting in a thick and creamy consistency. The haleem is typically garnished with fried onions, chopped mint leaves, coriander, and a drizzle of lemon juice.
